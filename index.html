The Colorado Family Farm

Locally Owned, Locally Produced Solar Energy Farm

Focusing on Research, Education, Pollinators & Art

We are excited to announce our plans to build a community solar garden in Boulder County. Our vision is to farm more than just solar energy – we’re also continuing the legacy my grandfather started when he began farming our land more than 45 years ago. We’re building a community-based, socially responsible enterprise that will supply locally produced energy to our Front Range neighbors, host a one-acre pollinator habitat, partner with research institutions to study crop growth under solar panels, and provide educational opportunities to our youth.
